Home-Fried Potatoes Recipe

Cooking time

🕓 30 min

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt

Cooking method

  1. Clean potatoes
  2. Rinse well under cold water
  3. In a pan pour oil and put on fire
  4. Cut potatoes and threw it into the pan
  5. Salt it and wait for the cooking
